User Manual

How to use this screen

Choose a ticker, optionally select a date, and click Analyze. The system will classify the market into three regimes and show a confidence score for each one.

What the regimes mean

  • Trending: the market is moving in one direction with persistence.
  • MeanReverting: price is oscillating and moving back toward an average.
  • HighVol: the market is noisy and risk is elevated.
  • LowVol: the market is calm and normal position sizing is suitable.
  • Bull: broad risk-on conditions are present.
  • Bear: broad risk-off conditions are present.

If the API shows an error, check that the Docker stack is running. If a regime looks weak, use the pipeline screen to inspect drift and retraining status.
